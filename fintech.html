FAQ

What’s different about Vention’s services?

We partner with companies of all sizes, no matter where they are in their journey. While we cut our teeth working with startups, we also work with more mature companies, so you get the best of both worlds, plus the knowledge that there’s no such thing as a “one-size-fits-all” solution and a nimble approach to every project. Also: We’ve been in business for 20+ years and have led the way in fintech long before it was commonly acknowledged as a sector.

What is your approach to compliance and regulation?

Vention is fluent in the many regulatory complexities that underpin financial institutions and fintech, including PCI DSS, CISP, and GDPR. Our job is to help you integrate compliance needs into a scalable solution, starting with design.

Does my company need to be a fintech service provider or financial institution to partner with Vention?

No. We view “fintech” broadly: It can mean anything from a custom payment system for a retailer (think of this as “embedded” fintech) to an app for a trading platform.

DealCloud

When DealCloud, a fintech platform that pairs CRM with deal management, needed to build scalable web and mobile apps for deal execution and on-the-go third-party data access, they turned to Vention. To update its aging IT system, leading online trading services provider StoneX turned to Vention. Pairing trading knowledge with mobile expertise, our developers worked in partnership with theirs to deliver a state-of-the-art website and mobile app that supports up to 20 million daily inquiries.
